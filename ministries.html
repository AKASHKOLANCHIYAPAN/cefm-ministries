Foundation of Faith

Statement of Faith

We believe the Absolute Authority and Divine Verbal Inspiration of the Old and New Testaments as the Word of God. There is but one living and true God, and in the Godhead, there are three Persons, equal in power and glory — God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Ghost.

Salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone without the addition of human works. But work with gratitude for the great salvation is expected. The Substitutionary Death of the Lord Jesus Christ and His Resurrection as the only way of Salvation through Faith.

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in Jesus Christ and the invisible and visible personal return of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Serving the Lord

Our Ministry Areas

The VISION of our Mission is HIS MISSION and not organisation. We as a church are part of the various ministries listed below.

⛪ Church Planting

Establishing new congregations in unreached villages and towns across India. Strengthening the roots of faith by planting over 162 churches.

📖 Beraya Bible Seminary

Equipping and empowering the next generation. Intensive missionary Bible training for those called to serve God in the mission field.

🤝 Helping the Underprivileged

Demonstrating Christ's love through action. Supporting widows, orphans, and the destitute through vital social welfare programs.

🌍 Outreach Ministries

Reaching the unreached across India, sharing the Gospel in deep villages and towns, offering spiritual and material hope.

🧒 Sunday School & VBS

Nurturing faith in children. engaging the youth through dynamic lessons, worship songs, and Vacation Bible Schools.

🔥 Youth Camp

Igniting a passion for Christ among young adults. Empowering them through spiritual gatherings, worship, and leadership training.

👨‍🏫 Pastor's Conference

Strengthening the shepherds of the flock. Providing regular spiritual nourishment, biblical teaching, and fellowship to our leaders.

🌊 Baptism

A public declaration of faith in Jesus Christ. We regularly conduct baptismal services to welcome new believers into the body of Christ.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Mission Get-togethers

Fostering unity among our workers. Regular gatherings for missionary families to share testimonies, pray, and find renewed encouragement.
