Foundation of Faith

Statement of Faith

We believe the Absolute Authority and Divine Verbal Inspiration of the Old and New Testaments as the Word of God. There is but one living and true God, and in the Godhead, there are three Persons, equal in power and glory — God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Ghost.

Salvation is by grace through faith in Christ alone without the addition of human works. But work with gratitude for the great salvation is expected. The Substitutionary Death of the Lord Jesus Christ and His Resurrection as the only way of Salvation through Faith.

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in Jesus Christ and the invisible and visible personal return of our Lord Jesus Christ.

What We Do

Our Ministry Areas

The VISION of our Mission is HIS MISSION and not organisation. We as a church are part of the various ministries listed below. Click on any ministry to learn more.

1

Church Planting

▼

CEFM is committed to establishing new congregations in unreached villages and towns across India. Our missionaries identify areas where the Gospel has not yet reached, build relationships with local communities, and plant indigenous churches that become self-sustaining centres of worship and spiritual growth. Through church planting, we have established over 162 churches across 7 states.

2

Literature Ministry

▼

The Literature Ministry publishes and distributes Gospel tracts, books, and magazines to spread the Word of God in local languages. These materials serve as powerful tools for evangelism, edification, and discipleship. Through printed literature, we reach people who may not have access to a church or a Bible, planting seeds of faith in countless lives.

3

Beraya Bible Seminary

▼

The Beraya Bible Seminary provides intensive missionary Bible training for both men and women who are called to serve God in the mission field. The curriculum covers theology, evangelism, church planting strategies, pastoral care, and cross-cultural ministry. Graduates are equipped and sent to serve as missionaries across India.

4

North India Ministry

▼

The North India Ministry is focused on Gospel outreach to Hindi-speaking states and other unreached regions of North India. Our missionaries serve in states such as Madhya Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, Bihar, Haryana, Delhi, and Rajasthan, overcoming language and cultural barriers to share the love of Christ with communities that have never heard the Gospel.

5

Global Evangelism

▼

CEFM's vision extends beyond India to reach nations worldwide with the Gospel. Through partnerships with international mission agencies and cross-cultural outreach programmes, we aim to fulfil the Great Commission — "Go ye into all the world, and preach the Gospel to every creature" (Mark 16:15).

6

Sunday School & VBS

▼

Our Sunday School and Vacation Bible School programmes nurture the next generation in the knowledge of God. Through engaging Bible lessons, songs, activities, and children's camps, we invest in the spiritual foundation of young lives. These programmes run across all our churches, impacting hundreds of children every year.

7

Hospital Ministry

▼

Our Hospital Ministry team regularly visits government and private hospitals to pray for the sick, offer words of encouragement, and share the hope of Jesus Christ. Volunteers provide emotional support, distribute Bibles and Gospel literature, and help connect patients with local churches for continued pastoral care.

8

Youth Camp

▼

Annual youth gatherings bring together young believers from across our churches for spiritual growth, fellowship, worship, and leadership training. Through powerful messages, workshops, and team activities, the Youth Camp equips the next generation to become bold witnesses for Christ in their communities.

9

Pastor's Conference

▼

CEFM organises annual Pastor's Conferences to equip, encourage, and strengthen pastors and church leaders serving in the mission field. These gatherings provide Biblical teaching, prayer support, fellowship with other leaders, and practical training in church administration, evangelism strategies, and spiritual leadership.

10

Open Air Gospel Meetings

▼

Open Air Gospel Meetings are public evangelistic gatherings held in villages and towns where the Gospel is preached to the local community. These outdoor worship services include singing, testimonies, and powerful Gospel messages. They serve as effective platforms for reaching people who may never enter a church building.

11

Outreach Ministries

▼

Community outreach programmes serve both the physical and spiritual needs of the people. Our teams visit remote villages to distribute food, clothing, and essential supplies while sharing the Gospel. These outreach initiatives build trust with local communities and open doors for further ministry and church planting.

12

Helping the Underprivileged

▼

CEFM actively supports differently-abled individuals, widows, orphans, and the most vulnerable members of society. Through financial assistance, food distribution, skill development programmes, and compassionate care, we extend the love of Christ to those who are often overlooked and forgotten.

13

Gospel Crusades

▼

Large-scale evangelistic crusades and revival meetings bring together believers and seekers for powerful Gospel proclamation. These events feature anointed preaching, prayer for healing and deliverance, and mass evangelism. Gospel Crusades have been instrumental in bringing thousands to faith in Christ across India and beyond.

14

Counselling

▼

Our Counselling Ministry offers Biblical guidance and pastoral care to people of all ages facing personal, family, or spiritual challenges. Trained counsellors provide confidential support rooted in Scripture, helping individuals find healing, hope, and direction through the wisdom of God's Word.
