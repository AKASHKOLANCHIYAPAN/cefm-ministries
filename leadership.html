National Leadership

Servants of the Most High

Under divine guidance, these anointed leaders steer the course of CEFM Ministries with unwavering faith and sacrificial love.

Apostle C. Selvaraj

Founder & Visionary

Converted from a Hindu background, Apostle C. Selvaraj received a divine calling to reach the perishing souls of India. In 1997, he founded CEFM with a bold vision to send 1,000 missionaries and plant 1,000 churches across the nation. In the past 27 years, through his unwavering faith and sacrificial commitment, CEFM has sent 280 missionaries, established 162 churches, and constructed 111 church buildings across 7 states of India.

Rev. A. Simeon Jebaraj

General Secretary

Rev. A. Simeon Jebaraj serves as the General Secretary of CEFM Ministries, overseeing the administration and coordination of 280+ missionaries across India. His daily Bible words and sermons on CEFM Live have blessed thousands of believers. He leads with humility, wisdom, and a deep passion for the Great Commission.

Rev. S. Hubert Subinraj

vice president

Rev. S. Hubert Subinraj serves as the Joint Secretary of CEFM Ministries, faithfully supporting the administrative and spiritual leadership of the mission. His dedicated service in coordinating missionary activities and church planting initiatives across multiple states has been instrumental in advancing the vision of CEFM to the unreached regions of India.
