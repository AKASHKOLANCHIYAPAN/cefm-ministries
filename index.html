The Vision

Apostle C. Selvaraj

Founder & Visionary of CEFM Ministries

"Called by the Divine Grace of our Lord Jesus Christ, CEFM Ministries stands as a beacon of hope for the unreached. Through fervent prayer, church planting, and compassionate social service, we seek to transform lives for the Kingdom of God."

Converted from a Hindu background, Apostle C. Selvaraj received a divine calling to reach the perishing souls of India. In 1997, he founded CEFM with a bold vision: to send 1,000 missionaries and plant 1,000 churches across the nation. In the past 27 years, CEFM has sent 280 missionaries, established 162 churches, and constructed 111 church buildings across 7 states. His legacy continues to inspire generations of workers for the Kingdom.
