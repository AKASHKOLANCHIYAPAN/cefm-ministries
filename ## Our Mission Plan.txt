## Our Mission Plan
#### 500 Missionaries
To the 232 districts of the Hindi region — Madhya Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, Bihar, Haryana, Delhi, Rajasthan.

#### 200 Missionaries
To the other states of India where the Gospel is yet to reach.

#### 300 Missionaries
To the very deep villages of South Indian states where the Gospel has not yet reached.

## Our Ministry Areas
The VISION of our Mission is HIS MISSION and not organisation. We as a church are part of the various ministries listed below.

#### Church Planting
Establishing new congregations in unreached villages and towns across India. Over 162 churches planted across 7 states.

#### Beraya Bible Seminary
Intensive missionary Bible training for men and women called to serve God in the mission field.

#### North India Ministry
Gospel outreach to Hindi-speaking states and unreached regions of North India.

#### Sunday School & VBS
Nurturing the next generation through Bible lessons, songs, activities, and children's camps.

#### Youth Camp
Annual gatherings for spiritual growth, fellowship, worship, and leadership training for young believers.

#### Pastor's Conference
Equipping and strengthening pastors and church leaders through Biblical teaching, prayer, and practical training.

#### Outreach Ministries
Serving the physical and spiritual needs of remote villages through food, clothing, and Gospel sharing.

#### Helping the Underprivileged
Supporting widows, orphans, and the vulnerable through financial assistance, food distribution, and skill development.

## Our Services
Special services and events that strengthen our faith and bring glory to God.
