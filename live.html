Daily Bread

Daily Bible Word

Rev. S. Simeon Jebaraj shares a daily word of encouragement and scriptural insight to nourish your spirit.

"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to Him, and He will make your paths straight."

— Proverbs 3:5-6

Dear brothers and sisters, today the Lord reminds us that our understanding is limited, but His wisdom is infinite. When life's path seems unclear, when decisions weigh heavy on your heart — surrender everything to Him. He who created the universe from nothing is more than able to guide your steps. Trust is not the absence of doubt; it is choosing to believe in God's goodness despite the uncertainty. Let us walk by faith today, not by sight.

— Rev. S. Simeon Jebaraj, General Secretary
